Are you good at time estimetion?

Time is money

Why again

  • To sell your time to a client
  • The list of tasks should be known and final
  • To plan your or other's work

Types of estimation

  • Preliminary estimation — hight-level estimation
  • Accurate estimation — low-level estimation
  • Revaluation of timings — something has changed (specifications, team etc.)

Common mistakes

  • Lack of docs or insufficient list of tasks
  • Risks are not included
  • It's hard to estimate new things: technologies, third-party API etc.
  • People do mistakes
  • Every developer is good (and bad) at something
  • Low personal responsibility

How to learn

0. Track your time

  • The 20 Best Time-Tracking Apps (article)

1. Make everything clear

  • Aks questions
  • Find all bottlenecks
  • Give best and worst estimations instead of an exact number

2. Be more specific

  • Decompose high-level tasks
  • Think of the (exact) implementation
  • Do not decompose everything

3. Think together

  • Assign time estimations to a few developers
  • Do time estimation reviews

4. Re-think your timings

  • Confirm an estimation before you start to work on the feature
  • Discuss excact implementation with the team

5. Make risks visible

  • Disclose a particularly unpredictible tasks
  • Do research before implementing 'unknown' tasks
  • Everyone in a team should know all risks
  • Don't forget of revaluation of timings

6. Do not forget about youself

  • Remember, you don't spend 100% of your working hours on coding
    • Reading docs, integrations
    • Деплой, git
    • Deploy, git, code reviews
    • Meetings
    • Testing
    • Coffee/cookies, emails, small talks, procrastination

7. Scream when it's crucial

  • Don't be afraid of asking for help
  • Inform your team/PM/client about time issues as soon as you get the idea you'll have them
  • Team/PM/client are humans and you could overpersuade them to make a different decision/timings/etc.

8. Analyze your work

  • Time estimations is not only about the future
  • Анализируйте оцененное и реально затраченное время
  • Проводите "ретро" по переоценным или недооцененным задачам
  • Накапливайте опыт для последующих оценок
  • Учитывайте коэффициент погрешности оценок (реальное / оцененное)

Bonus. Повышение прогнозируемости

  • Улучшайте комуникацию в команде
  • Как только появилась задержки по срокам - сразу к менеджеру
  • Комбинируйте людей в команды так, чтобы им было комфортно
  • Группируйте людей (по отделам, по проектам)
  • Пробуйте различные техники программирования (парное, экстремальное)
